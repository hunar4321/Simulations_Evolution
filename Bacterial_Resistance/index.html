Demo of Bacterial Resistance

  • most antibiotics kill bacteria by affecting their membranes
  • bacteria vary in their wall thickness, and hence, vary in their resistance to antibiotics
  • use left mouse click to adminster antibiotics
  • use space-bar to simulate bacterial re-generation
  • adminster antibiotics and follow it by re-generation. Do that repeatedly and see how bacterial resistances emerges as the antibiotic kills the weaker bacteria letting the stronger bacteria (those with thicker walls) re-generate
